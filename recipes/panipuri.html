Pani Puri Recipe

Description:

Panipuri or golgappa is a deep fried breaded hollow spherical shell about 1 in in diameter.

It is filled with a combination of potatoes, raw onions, chickpeas, and spices. It is a common snack and street food in the Indian subcontinent.

Ingredient List:

  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1.5 tbsp Chopped Coriander
  • 1 tsp roasted Cumin Powder
  • 1 tsp Chaat Masala Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • As required Black Salt or Pink or Regular
